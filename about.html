About

Meggie Royer. Female. 23 and in love with words.
"I did not expect to be me for an entire lifetime."
-Don Nace

Meggie Royer is an author and artist whose work tends to address hard and often unspoken topics—real and contemporary issues—including suicide, abortion, rape, gender identities, and mental health.

Royer perfectly captures the reality and emotions faced within these topics. Although not all are trials she has faced herself, Royer uses stories of those close to her to make their voices heard and let others know they are not alone. Through her work, Royer provides a sense of hope while raising awareness to the issues within our society.

Raised in Ames, Iowa with her triplet brother and sister, Royer has held a successful career, recieving numerous awards, publishing several books, and frequently has her work used to help others through trials. Please use this website to read work by Meggie Royer, purchase her books, get tips or help, or send a message.
